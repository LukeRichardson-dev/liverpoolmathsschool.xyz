University of Liverpool Mathematics School

Developing promising young mathematicians from across the Liverpool City Region

Our Maths School is the perfect environment for talented sixth formers to open their minds to new challenges. Students learn and thrive together following our stimulating academic curriculum centred on A-level Maths, Further Maths, Computer Science and Physics. Founded by, and drawing upon the expertise of, the University of Liverpool our curriculum is built on mathematical A-Levels but goes well beyond the A-Level specifications to create a broad-based and coherent foundation for life in general and advanced learning of mathematical science in particular.

We exist to enable children with a strong interest and high potential in the mathematical sciences to achieve global impact through careers in the mathematical sciences.  By preparing them thoroughly and comprehensively for STEM degrees and significant roles both within local communities and the global community of science, technology, engineering and mathematics, our students will be equipped to become tomorrow’s industrial and academic researchers, innovators who will address the world’s greatest challenges, teachers who will inspire the next generation, wealth creators, entrepreneurs and problem solvers.
