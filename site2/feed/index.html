]]> August 2022: Our first set of A-Level Results https://liverpoolmathsschool.org/august-2022-our-first-set-of-a-level-results/ Thu, 18 Aug 2022 12:00:10 +0000 https://liverpoolmathsschool.org/?p=1831 We are  delighted to share our first ever A-Level results:

  • The average grade across all entries was A

  • Over one third (36%) of students achieved 3 A* or A grades

  • 87% of A-Level entries achieved A*, A or B grades

  • 59% of entries achieved A* or A grades

  • 20% of entries achieved A* grades

  • A-Level Mathematics: 96% A*-B, 76% A*-A

  • A-Level Computer Science: 88% A*-B, 65% A*-A

  • A-Level Physics: 83% A*-B, 52% A*-A

  • A-Level Further Mathematics: 80% A*-B, 45% A*-A

  • 56% of students achieved at least AAB in facilitating subjects

We’re absolutely delighted with these outcomes which reflect the huge amount of hard work that students have put in over the last two years.  Almost all students are now set up with a good STEM degree course at university, or as part of a Degree Apprenticeship.
